Welcome!

With the soothing breeze from the lush green hills behind, Breeze Valley is a nature-friendly experience set in an exceptionally beautiful landscape. Overlooking the dense rainforests, the place offers splendid views and a calm, cool and relaxing ambience. Rooms designed with elegant charming interior having exquisite views of the surrounding hills so as to provide a luxurious experience indoor and outdoor. Expect very clean en suite bathrooms and super comfortable beds. Breakfast is included.

We are a family run hotel with the mission of providing a home away from home experience for our guests.We would love to make your stay and experience most memorable. We hope to welcome you soon in our little slice of paradise
